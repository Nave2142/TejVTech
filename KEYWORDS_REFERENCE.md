## üöÄ Keyword Strategy Tips

### Best Practices
1. **Use long-tail keywords** - Less competition, higher intent
2. **Target specific services** - "web development" not "internet"
3. **Include modifiers** - "best", "affordable", "custom"
4. **Focus on intent** - What is the user looking for?
5. **Monitor competitors** - Track their keywords
6. **Update regularly** - Keywords change over time
7. **Combine variations** - Singular, plural, related terms
8. **Think about CTR** - Make titles clickable

### Common Mistakes to Avoid
‚ùå Keyword stuffing (overusing keywords)
‚ùå Ignoring search intent
‚ùå Targeting only high-volume keywords
‚ùå Not using long-tail keywords
‚ùå Forgetting variations (plural, synonyms)
‚ùå Targeting irrelevant keywords
‚ùå Not tracking rankings
‚ùå Using same keywords on multiple pages
